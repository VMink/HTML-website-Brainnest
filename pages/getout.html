Get Out!

Take me back.

Synopsis

Chris is a young and successful African-American photographer who, for five months, has been dating Rose, a white girl.
Although he is not very convinced, he ends up agreeing to Rose's invitation to meet her family, so she invites him to spend a weekend in the country with her parents, Missy and Dean.
At first, Chris thinks that the parents' complacent behavior is due to their nervousness about their daughter's interracial relationship, but as the hours pass, everything changes.
