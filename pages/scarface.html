Scarface

Synopsis

Tony Montana is a cold and bloodthirsty Cuban emigrant who settles in Miami with the aim of becoming a major gangster.
With the collaboration of his friend Manny Rivera, he begins a brilliant criminal career with the aim of gaining access to the leadership of a drug organization.
