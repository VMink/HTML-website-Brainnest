Pinnochio

Take me back.

Synopsis

Gepetto the carpenter (David Bradley) lives completely alone and isolated in 1930s Italy, while fascism is growing around him.
Tired of this situation, he decides to change it himself and create a son of wood that he will call Pinocchio (Gregory Mann).
One night, thanks to a bit of magic, the wooden doll comes to life and fulfills all of Gepetto's dreams of having a little son.
However, Pinocchio is not what the carpenter expects, instead of being obedient and calm, the boy loves fun and mischief.
Pinocchio is fed up with not being what his father wants, so he goes off on adventures, but soon realizes he's gone too far.
